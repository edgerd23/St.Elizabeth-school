About Our School

Discover our history, philosophy, and dedicated team

Our Story

Founded in 1995, St. Elizabeth Nursery School has been a cornerstone of early childhood education in our community for over 25 years. We began with a simple mission: to provide a safe, nurturing environment where children can explore, learn, and grow.

Our Philosophy

We believe in a child-centered approach to education that respects each child's unique developmental path. Our curriculum combines structured learning with ample opportunities for creative play, fostering both academic skills and social-emotional growth.

Our Values

Safety, respect, creativity, and community are the core values that guide everything we do. We're committed to creating an inclusive environment where every child feels valued and empowered to reach their full potential.
